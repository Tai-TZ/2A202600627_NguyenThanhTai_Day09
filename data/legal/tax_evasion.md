# Tax Evasion and IRS Penalties

**Keywords:** tax, evasion, irs, penalty, fraud, revenue, thuế

Tax evasion (26 U.S.C. § 7201): felony with up to $250K fine and 5 years prison.
Civil fraud penalty: 75% of underpayment (IRC § 6663).
Failure to file: up to $25K fine and 1 year prison.
IRS can assess back taxes + interest going back 6 years (unlimited for fraud).
Officers may be personally liable as responsible persons.

FBAR penalties: up to $100K or 50% of account balance per violation.
FATCA non-compliance: 30% withholding on US-source payments.
