# Data Privacy — GDPR, CCPA

**Keywords:** data, privacy, gdpr, ccpa, consent, dữ liệu, breach

Sharing user data without consent violates:
- CCPA: fines up to $7,500 per intentional violation
- GDPR: fines up to 4% of global revenue or EUR 20M
- FTC Act Section 5 for unfair/deceptive practices

Data breach notification required within 72 hours under GDPR.
Class action exposure under state privacy laws ($100-$750 per consumer).
