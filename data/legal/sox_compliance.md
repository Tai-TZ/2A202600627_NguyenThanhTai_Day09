# SOX and Regulatory Compliance

**Keywords:** sox, sarbanes, compliance, sec, regulation, fcpa, aml

SOX violations:
- CEO/CFO false certification — up to $5M fine, 20 years prison (§ 906)
- Record destruction — up to 20 years (§ 802)
- Whistleblower retaliation — up to 10 years (§ 1107)

FCPA anti-bribery: up to $250K fine per violation (individuals), $2M (corporations).
SEC can bar individuals from serving as officers or directors.
